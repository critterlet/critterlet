Frequently Asked Questions

What premium features do I get?

Premium unlocks 8 course slots (vs 3 free), custom study timers, advanced analytics with month/year views, unlimited flashcards, exclusive premium critters like unicorns and dragons, and regular bonus paw point rewards.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time through your device settings or account page. You'll continue to have premium access until the end of your current billing period.

What's the difference between plans?

All plans include the same premium features. Monthly is $4.99/month, Yearly saves you 33% at $39.99/year, and Lifetime is a one-time $99.99 payment with no recurring charges.

Do you offer student discounts?

We're working on student verification and discounts! Contact us at critterlet@gmail.com with your student email for early access to educational pricing.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! New users get a 7-day free trial of all premium features. No credit card required to start your trial.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your study data is always yours! If you cancel premium, you keep all your flashcards, progress, and critters. You'll just be limited to 3 courses and lose access to premium features.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any differences.

Are there family or group plans?

Family and group plans are coming soon! Join our mailing list or follow us on social media for updates on multi-user pricing.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help you choose the perfect plan for your study needs!
