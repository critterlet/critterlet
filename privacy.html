Overview

Critterlet (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a study app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use Critterlet.

Information We Collect

Account data (name, email), usage data (study streaks, session length), device diagnostics (app version, OS, crash logs), and optional study content you create (flashcards, to-dos, notes).

How We Use Information

We use your information to provide and improve features, sync your content, maintain security, deliver support, and communicate updates.

Sharing and Processing

We use service providers (cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting). We do not sell your information.

Data Retention

We retain data while you have an account and as needed for legal or business purposes. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your data by emailing critterlet@gmail.com.

Children

Not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

International Users

We process data in Canada and other countries and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and applicable laws.

Security

We employ reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this policy and will post updates here.
